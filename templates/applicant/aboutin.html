    About Us

    There is great potential for growth and improvement when it comes to recruiting Black individuals. The numbers speak for themselves. We aim to eradicate the disadvantages that disincentevize hiring Black applicants.

    • The first barrier to entry for Black applicants are GPA cutoffs. A study done by Handshake shows that Black applicants are cut off from applying for a job due to the GPA cutoffs one and a half times more than a non-minority applicant.
    • Second, complicated application processes can deter Black individuals from applying for a position. The same Handshake study shows that the more laborious a job application is, the less likely Black applicants are to apply. Specifically, if all that is required for a job application is a resume, two and half times more Black applicants will apply for the job.
    • The third hurdle for Black candidates is ambiguity with regards to salary and relocation assistance in job listings. Thirty six percent more Black applicants will apply for a job when there is relocation assistance mentioned in the job description, and even just listing the annual salary on the job description will increase the chance of a Black candidate applying for the job by thirteen percent.
    • Lastly, strict recruiting windows limit black applicants. There is a six percent disparity between black and non black applicants in applying for jobs during the peak recruiting seasons, which then translates to less black applicants hired.
    At Black Cyber Recruiter, our mission is to provide opportunities for employers to connect with Black candidates and diminish the disparity between non-POC persons in the work force. Additionally, we aim to assist Black candidates in their search for work by leveraging AI and recommending the opportunities with the best fit for each individual applicant.
